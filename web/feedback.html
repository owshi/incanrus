About the company Incanrus I discovered quite by accident, through my friend who for several years lived in Canada. In my plans have long been an issue about getting education abroad. I didn’t know where to start, could not decide which country to go to, what school to choose which generally demands. I was lucky that I ended up in the hands of professionals who helped me to make the right choice, execute all necessary documents and obtain a visa and leave. Like any person departing to another country without knowing anything about the school, residence and generally how life in a foreign land I had certain fears and doubts. But I am very happy with the result of the command Incanrus. I arrived at all ready. I met my Canadian family that helped me overcome the culture shock and introduced me to the culture of Canada. Now I am studying at the language school IH Vancouver. In my opinion the school meets the highest standards. Good location, friendly staff, excellent qualified international student group, new equipment. For half a year in Vancouver, I can’t believe how quickly I learned English, how many saw and learned. Canada is a wonderful country and I am glad that I finally ended up here. In the school I left to study another six months, after which I plan to go to College. Want to say a huge thanks to Incanrus for for supporting the implementation of my plans.
